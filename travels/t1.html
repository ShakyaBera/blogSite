Day 1

On day one, we woke up at 4:00 am in the morning, because we had our flight at 9:05 am. We got ready boarded our cab and reached the airport. There we performed all the check in processes. After that, we had an hour to wait and nothing to do. So I started window shopping. Like this, I wasted a good amount of time and got ready for boarding our plane. Sadly I wasn't able to take pictures of the plane, or pictures inside the plane. Then we landed on Bagdogra, our driver was waiting for us. We were quite lucky and got a Tata Sumo GOLD (it is -24 Karat gold). Now see the video for the experiencing my journey to Lamahatta.

On the way we halted at Teesta Triveni. It is the meeting point of Rangeet and Teesta River. It is a beach type place, which is covered with rocks. The place is amazing. Try to absorb the beauty of the place with the help of the video.

Next we went to the Lovers Meet View Point. This is just 4 kms above the Teesta Triveni. It is a top down view of the meeting point. And this is the place where we ate momos, to our hearts content. They were really cheap and tasty also. The view point was very nice and from top, the distinct colors of the two rivers were clearly visible. See the video, go on.

Then we headed for the abode of orange orchards and pine gardens, Lamhatta, i.e the 9th Mile. After reaching the place, a we arrived at a state of perfect happiness. The view was literally awesome. Just see the video... ( Am I not able to write or the place is to beautiful to be described, Haha :) )

Day 2

We started the day with a walk to the Lamhatta Ecotourism park. On the ticket counter we saw a stone incription. Salute to the Martyrs !

After getting the tickets we headed for the sacred lake, which was uphill, and we had to wake for about 40 minutes. It was quiet tiresome. Here I am giving some pictures of park and lake. The lake was amazing. The look you get after a lengthy walk is awesome.
